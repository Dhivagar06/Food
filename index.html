Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is a well-loved Indian dish known for its succulent pieces of chicken that are marinated in a spiced yogurt mixture, grilled or roasted until tender and then simmered in a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce.

Paneer Tikka

Paneer Tikka is a beloved Indian appetizer or snack that features chunks of paneer (a type of Indian cottage cheese) marinated in a mixture of yogurt and various spices, such as cumin, coriander, and garam masala.

Rogan Josh

Rogan Josh is a flavorful and aromatic dish that originates from Kashmir, India. It features tender pieces of mutton (goat or lamb) slow-cooked in a rich and spicy gravy made with a blend of aromatic spices, including dried red chilies, cloves, and cinnamon
